Welcome to the Project on Electric Cars

What is an Electric Car?

An electric car (EV) is a motor vehicle that is powered by an electric motor and runs on electricity. The motor turns the generator to produce electricity that is then used to power the vehicle.

Benefits of Electric Cars

  • Zero tailpipe emissions, reducing air pollution
  • No dependency on foreign oil
  • Reduced maintenance and insurance costs
  • Lower operating costs compared to traditional internal combustion engine (ICE) vehicles

Examples of Electric Car Brands

Some well-known electric car brands include Tesla, Nissan, and BMW.
